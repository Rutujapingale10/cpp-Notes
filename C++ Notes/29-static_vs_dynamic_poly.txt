Static polymorphism -
       Type of polymorphism that collect the information to call
a method during compile time.

It occure at compile and also called static binding and early binding

Its execution speed is high
method overloading is example of static binding.


Dynamic Polymorphism - 
A type of polymorphism that collects information to call a method at 
a run time.
It occure at runtime
It known as dynamic binding or late binding
Execution speed is slow.
Method overriding is example of dynamic binding
